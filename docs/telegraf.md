[PSI](https://github.com/gridscale/linux-psi-telegraf-plugin/blob/main/README.md)
Pressure Stall Information (PSI) is a feature in modern Linux kernels (starting from version 4.20) designed to provide insight into resource contention on a system. It measures how much time the system spends waiting for CPU, memory, or I/O resources to become available due to contention or pressure.
